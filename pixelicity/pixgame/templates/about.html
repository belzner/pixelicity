Welcome to Pixelicity!
Hi there! I'm Mayr, the Mayor of Pixelicity. Well, Mayor is a strong word. Really, I just happened across this bit of land one day and decided it was mine now. Nothing wrong with that, right? I've always wanted to own a city!

It's looking a bit sparse though. Maybe you can help with that?

Construct

When you check in at a location in the real world, the corresponding building appears in Pixelicity. Grow the city to unlock new buildings and entice Pixens to visit.

Collect

As you grow your city, you'll collect achievements to mark your progress. Some are straightforward. Some... not so much!

Explore

Get to know the place you live! By exploring your city, you can help Pixelicity grow. At a loss for where to go next? Pixelicity gives you suggestions and challenges to get you going.
