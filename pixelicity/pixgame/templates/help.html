What is this all about?

Pixelicity is a website designed to encourage you to explore the place you live. Alongside Mayr and some other quirky characters, you'll work to bring the city from a sparse wasteland to a bustling metropolis.

So how do I play?

Start by making an account through the link at the upper right corner of the screen. Mayr will guide you through building your first building, but from there it's your turn! You can fill in any places you've already been by clicking on the circular marker at that spot on the map (mouse over the marker to see the name of the location). Then, anytime you visit a new location, add it on Pixelicity. Over time, you'll see the city grow before your very eyes!

How do I tell how I'm doing?

As you grow your city, you'll collect achievements for reaching certain goals. You can see which achievements you have, and which you have yet to get, on the "Achievements" panel on the right side of the page.

You might also want a more direct way to track your progress. The "Statistics" panel on the left side of the page tells you how many buildings you've built (and of what types), how many achievements you've collected, and more.

Help! Something isn't working!

Sometimes there may be bugs in the site that we haven't caught yet. If you see something that doesn't work properly, please email pixelicity@mit.edu.
